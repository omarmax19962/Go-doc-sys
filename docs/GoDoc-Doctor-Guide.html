Section 1

Getting started

Go Doc is a web app — there is nothing to install. It works on any phone, tablet or computer browser, and you can add it to your home screen so it opens like a normal app.

1 · Open the app

Go to go-doc-sys.vercel.app in your browser (Safari or Chrome). To keep it handy, tap Share → Add to Home Screen — an icon appears just like a regular app.

2 · Sign in on the Doctor tab

a
On the sign-in screen, make sure the Doctor tab is selected (not Admin).
b
Enter the email and password your admin gave you (see the box below).
c
Tap Sign in. You'll land on your personal dashboard.

🔑 Your login details

Your admin creates your account before your first day. You'll receive:

  • Email — usually yourname@godoc.com
  • Temporary password — change it after your first sign-in (ask your admin to reset it for you anytime).

Only your admin can register a new doctor. If the app says "Ask your admin to add you first," your email hasn't been added yet.

🔔 Turn on notifications

The first time you sign in, tap Allow when the browser asks about notifications, then tap the small bell-enable button at the top of your screen. This is how you'll be alerted about new bookings, schedule changes and approvals — even when the app is closed.

Your home screen — the four tabs

Everything you do lives under four tabs at the top of the screen.

VisitsToday's sessions
CalendarYour week
PatientsYour caseload
AvailabilityWhen & where

🗓️ Visits

Your upcoming and in-progress sessions. Each card shows the date & time, patient name, zone and diagnosis. Tap a card to open the session and document it. Use “Log a session” at the top to record a session you did independently (e.g. a walk-in or an extra visit).

📅 Calendar

A day-by-day view of your schedule. Tap any empty slot — or the “Add patient to slot” button — to book a patient into that time. Tap an existing appointment to open that patient's file.

👥 Patients

Everyone assigned to you. Each entry shows their status, zone, diagnosis and how many sessions they've had. Tap to view the full history — past SOAP notes, diagnosis and files.

⚙️ Availability

Two things you control yourself:

  • Weekly availability — tap the times you can see patients. Saves instantly; the admin sees it live.
  • Coverage zones — tap the areas you cover (search in Arabic or English). The admin only books you where you work.

Keep these two up to date

Your availability and zones are how the admin knows when and where to book you. If your schedule changes, update it here first — it takes five seconds and prevents double-bookings.

Documenting a session (SOAP note)

After every visit, file a short note so the record stays complete and the admin can bill correctly.

1
Open the session — from Visits, tap the patient's card; or tap “Log a session” to start a fresh one.
2
Fill in the SOAP note — Subjective, Objective, Assessment and Plan. You can pull exercises and modalities from the built-in library.
3
Set the diagnosis if it isn't recorded yet — this shows on the patient's card for everyone.
4
Submit. The session is marked complete, and the admin is notified the note is filed.

⏳ Booking a follow-up? It needs admin approval

When you schedule the next session from inside a note — or add a patient to a slot from your Calendar — it's created as a doctor-added appointment and marked Pending until the admin approves it. You'll see “⏳ Pending admin approval” on the appointment, and it becomes Approved once they confirm. This keeps the master schedule accurate.

Rescheduling or cancelling

On any upcoming visit you'll find quick actions to reschedule or remove it. The admin is notified of the change automatically.

بالعربي — توثيق الجلسة

بعد كل جلسة، افتحها من تبويب Visits واكتب ملاحظة الـ SOAP (الأعراض، الفحص، التقييم، الخطة) وحدّد التشخيص لو مش متسجل، وبعدين اضغط Submit. لو حجزت جلسة متابعة بنفسك، هتظهر “بانتظار موافقة الأدمن” لحد ما يأكدها.

Section 4

Patient statuses & good habits

Each patient carries a status so the whole team knows where they stand.

StatusWhat it means
BookedScheduled, first session not done yet.
ActiveIn treatment — seeing them regularly.
PausedTemporarily on hold (travel, illness, etc.).
DischargedTreatment complete. The admin can reactivate them later if they return.

The admin manages status changes. Your job is to keep notes and diagnoses current — the status often follows from your documentation.

Frequently asked

I forgot my password.

Ask your admin to reset it — they can issue a new one in seconds.

It says my email isn't registered.

Your admin hasn't added you yet, or you typed a different email. Confirm the exact address with them.

I'm on the wrong screen / I see an Admin error.

Make sure you picked the Doctor tab on the sign-in screen, not Admin.

My new booking isn't on the master schedule.

That's expected — doctor-added bookings stay Pending until the admin approves them.

A patient came back after discharge.

Let the admin know — they can reactivate the patient and bring them back into the loop.

✅ Daily checklist

  • Check Visits at the start of your day.
  • File a SOAP note after every session.
  • Keep your availability and zones current.
  • Keep notifications on so you never miss a change.

Questions this guide didn't answer? Message your Go Doc admin — they can see everything live and help right away.
